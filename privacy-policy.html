1. Commitment to Data Confidentiality

At ShriraghavTech, we understand that your laptops, storage drives, and digital devices contain sensitive personal and professional data. We adhere to strict data protection standards during hardware diagnosis, chip micro-soldering, software troubleshooting, and data recovery operations.

2. Information We Collect

  • Contact Details: Your name, phone number, email address, and physical address provided during consultation or job ticket submission.
  • Device Diagnostic Metadata: Model numbers, serial numbers, hardware specifications, and reported technical symptoms necessary for repair tracking.
  • Restored Data Files: Temporary storage of recovered files during critical data recovery services, strictly retained only until verified and handed back to the owner.

3. Data Recovery & Hard Drive Security

All hard drives, SSDs, and storage devices entrusted to us for recovery are handled in isolated, cleanroom environments. Our technicians do not browse, copy, or retain customer documents, photos, or confidential files beyond the explicit scope required to verify recovery success. Temporary backup copies are permanently sanitized after client confirmation.
